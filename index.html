MSRP - Meta Search and RePlace

Presentation

MSRP is a utility to perform regular expression substitutions. It reads one or more files as input and modifies both their contents and filenames by renaming files if appropriate.

More about MSRP: msrp, a useful addition to your toolbox.

Get it
